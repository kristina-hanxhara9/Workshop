What you leave with

Not a course.
A skill.

Most AI workshops teach you about AI. This one teaches you how to use it specifically, the one skill that makes every AI tool significantly more useful immediately.

01
One framework
Role. Context. Output. Three things, in that order, every time. You'll use it from tomorrow morning and the habit compounds from there.
02
Five tools, clearly mapped
Copilot, Gemini, Perplexity, ChatGPT, Claude each built around a different kind of intelligence. You leave knowing exactly which one to open for which task.
03
One cost identified
Every person who has attended this session has left with one thing in their business they hadn't questioned before. A line item, a process, a recurring task. Worth examining.
04
Three rules that protect you
What not to share, when to verify, and where the accountability stays. The practical knowledge that keeps you safe while you move faster.
